亞歷山大鸚鵡
Alexandrine Parakeet
Psittacula eupatria
近危。留鳥。外地引入品種，全身綠色，嘴大紅色，虹膜黃色。頸上有紅領環，肩部紅色。飛行時像綠色的十字架，尾尖。常小群出現。見於市區公園.
Near Threatened. Resident. Introduced green parakeet with big red bill, yellow eyering and red neck and shoulder patch. In flight shows a “flying cross” profile with pointed tail. Occurs in small parties. Found in urban parks.
