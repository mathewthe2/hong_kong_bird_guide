中華仙鶲
Chinese Blue Flycatcher
Cyornis glaucicomans
無危。迷鳥。雄性前額亮藍色， 頭和上體深藍色，喉部和胸部橙色。 雌性上體暖棕色，淺黃色喉部，橙褐色胸部和棕色脇部。見於濃密的叢林下層和樹林。
Least Concern.Vagrant. Male has pale shining blue across forehead .Head and upperparts dark blue. Rufous-orange on throat and breast. Female has warm brown upperparts , pale buffish throat, orange-rufous breast and brownish flanks. Found in dense undergrowth and forest.
