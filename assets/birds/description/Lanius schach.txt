棕背伯勞
Long-tailed Shrike
Lanius schach
無危。留鳥。體型較大而尾長，羽色為褐、黑、灰、白四色配搭。明顯的黑色眼罩延伸至耳羽，頭部黑色。見於開闊原野和濕地.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Large-sized shrike with long-tail. Plumage with a combination of brown, black, grey and white. Prominent dark eyestripe extends to ear coverts. Grey head contrasts with rufous upperparts. Wings and tail black. Found in open areas and wetlands
