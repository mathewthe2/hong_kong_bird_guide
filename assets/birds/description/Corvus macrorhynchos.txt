大嘴烏鴉
Large-billed Crow
Corvus macrorhynchos
無危。留鳥。香港最常見的大型黑色烏鴉。全身黑色，嘴黑色而粗厚，與前額銜接成台階狀。叫聲爲嘹亮的「鴉—鴉—」聲。見於樹木疏落的山區和海岸.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Large corvid, black overall. Bill black and thick, steep angle with forehead. Call is a loud and clear "ar…ar…". The commonest crow in Hong Kong. Found in lightly wood areas and seacoasts.
