紅脇藍尾鴝
Red-flanked Bluetail
Tarsiger cyanurus
無危。春及冬季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。喉部白色、脇橙黃色，尾藍色，嘴及腳黑色。雄鳥上體鮮藍色，雌鳥及未成年幼鳥則為橄欖棕色；下體白色沾褐色。見於林地, 濕地及開闊原野.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. White throat, rufous-orange flanks and blue tails Black bill and legs. Adult male has bright blue upperparts while female and immature are olive-brown. Underparts are white with brown tint.  Found in woodlands, wetlands and open areas.
