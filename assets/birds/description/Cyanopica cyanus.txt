灰喜鵲
Azure-winged Magpie
Cyanopica cyanus
無危。留鳥。頭、嘴和腳黑色，上體淡褐灰色，飛羽和尾部天藍色，尾羽末端有白點。喉至下體淡白色，沾有褐灰色斑。見於林區,最近米埔有一引入種群。
Least Concern. Residnet. Head, bill and legs black. Upperparts pale brownish grey. Flight feathers and tail skyblue, with white at tip of tail. Throat to underparts buffy white, with pale brownish grey. Found in woodlands, recently introduced resident at Mai Po.
