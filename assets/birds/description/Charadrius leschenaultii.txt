鐵嘴沙鴴
Greater Sand Plover
Charadrius leschenaultii
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。體型較大的鴴。嘴黑色，較蒙古沙鴴長和粗；腳偏黃色且較長。繁殖羽頭頂前端至後枕沾栗色，胸帶栗色，較蒙古沙鴴纖細。見於后海灣一帶各類型濕地。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Large plover. Compared with Lesser Sand Plover, it has longer, thicker black bill, and longer yellowish legs. Found in various wetland habitats around the Deep Bay area.
