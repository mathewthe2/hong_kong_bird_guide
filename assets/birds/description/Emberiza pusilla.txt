小鵐
Little Bunting
Emberiza pusilla
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥,冬候鳥。小型的鵐。冠紋、眉紋和頰下紋粗而淡黃色，貫眼紋和冠側紋深褐色，面頰栗色。上體紅褐色，有深色的粗縱紋。下體白色，胸部和脇部有黑色縱紋。嘴灰色，腳淡紛紅色。見於任何開闊地區和大片蘆葦.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Small bunting with thick buffy crown stripe, supercilium and submoustachial stripe. Eyestripe and lateral crown stripes dark brown. Cheeks chestnut. Upperparts rufous brown, with thick and dark streaks. Underparts white with with black stripes on the breast and flanks. Grey bill and pink legs. Found in any open areas and reedbeds.
