長趾蛙
Three-striped Grass Frog
Hylarana macrodactyla
無危。細小的蛙類，身長不超過5厘米。身體修長，綠色或啡色，背部有三條金黃色條紋，由喙部伸延至肛門。條紋間有深色的斑點。吻部尖長。見於溪流、農田和池塘。
Least Concern. Small frog with total length of 5cm. Slender body in green or brown with 3 golden yellow stripes from snout to vent on the back. Dark spots between the stripes. Long and pointed snout. Found in streams, cultivated fields and ponds.
