斑頭鵂鶹
Asian Barred Owlet
Glaucidium cuculoides
無危。留鳥。頭部渾圓，虹膜黃色，嘴黃色。頭和上體深褐色而帶濃密淡褐色細橫紋，尾部深色。見於林地。
Least Concern. Common Residents. Round-headed. Yellow iris, yellow bill. Head and upperparts are dark-brown with dense pale brownish bars. Tail is dark and underparts are whitish with brown stripes. Found in woodlands.
