灰背燕尾
Slaty-backed Forktail
Enicurus schistaceus
無危。偶見鳥。嘴及面黑色，頭頂及背灰色，下體及腰白色，長尾黑白分明像剪刀，腳淡色。幼鳥的頭和背深褐色，胸部有鱗狀斑。見於河流附近。
Least Concern. Occasional Visitor. Black bill and face. Slaty-grey crown and mantle. White underparts and rump. Black and white long scissor-like tail. Pale legs. Immature birds have olive-brown on head and mantle with scaly patterns on breast. Never far from running water.
