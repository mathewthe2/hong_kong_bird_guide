紅喉姬鶲
Red-throated Flycatcher
Ficedula albicilla
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。體型細小的鶲，大致灰褐色，嘴黑色，下體白色。尾黑色，尾部經常上下擺動。喉部灰褐色，繁殖時中央部份會變為橙紅色。在林地和花園出現.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. A small greyish-brown flycatcher. Blackish bill. Underparts whitish. Black tail  Often flicks tail. Throat brownish-grey when not breeding, but turns to orange-red with grey on sides in breeding plumage. Found in woodlands and gardens.
