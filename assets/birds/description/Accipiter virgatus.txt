松雀鷹
Besra
Accipiter virgatus
無危。留鳥。小型猛禽。深灰褐色雄鳥有白色腹部且有褐色粗橫紋。雌鳥顔色較深褐，胸前有明顯橫紋。幼鳥下體縱紋較多。見於林地。
Least Concern. Common Residents. Small raptor. Male is in dark greyish brown, has white belly with thick brown strips across. Female is darker brown in colour, with brown strips on breast. Juvenile has more stripes on underparts. Found in woodlands.
