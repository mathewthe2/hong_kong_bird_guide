中白鷺
Intermediate Egret
Ardea intermedia
無危。留鳥、春季秋季過境遷徙鳥及冬候鳥。中型鷺鳥，全身白色，腳全黑，嘴黃色，嘴尖沾黑色。外貌和大白鷺相似，但嘴較短。見於后海灣一帶各類型濕地。
Least Concern. Common Residents. Medium-sized white egret with dusky-tipped yellow bill. Legs and toes are black in colour. Resembles with Great Egret, but with shorter bill. Found in various wetlands around the Deep Bay area.
