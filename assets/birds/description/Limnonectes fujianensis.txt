大頭蛙
Big-headed Frog
Limnonectes fujianensis
無危。中等身型的蛙類，身長5至7厘米。身體呈深啡或黑色，背部有突點。頭部特別大及呈三角形。背部或有倒轉的W字母的斑紋突起。見於森林和溪流。
Least Concern. Medium-sized frog with a length of 5-7 cm. Dark-brown or black body with bump on the back. Especially large and triangular head. An inverse "w" raised marking behind the eyes on the back. Inhabit in forest and streams.
