金眶鴴
Little Ringed Plover
Charadrius dubius
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥，留鳥。嘴黑色，鮮明黃色眼圈，喉部白色並有完整白色頸圈。繁殖期時有黑色胸帶，眼後白眉向上延伸至頭頂。非繁殖期時黑色胸帶變成褐色。見於后海灣濕地及開闊田野。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Winter Visitors, Common Residents. Black bill and prominent yellow eyering. White throat with complete white neck ring. In breeding plumage, it has complete black breast band and white supercilium behind the eyes and extending to forecrown. Black breast band turns brown in non-breeding season. Found in the Deep Bay wetlands and open fields.
