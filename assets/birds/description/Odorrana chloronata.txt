大綠蛙
Green Cascade Frog
Odorrana chloronata
無危。雌性可長達12.5厘米，雄性身長約6-7厘米。頭部及背部深綠色，身體兩旁褐色。足尖之間的蹼十分發達。有明顯的鼓膜。見於溪流。
Least Concern. Female can reach to 12.5cm, males grow to 6-7cm long. Dark-green head and back. Lateral side of body dark brown. Well-developed digits on toe pads. Shows obvious tympanum. Found in streams.
