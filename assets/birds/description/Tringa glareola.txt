Wood Sandpiper
Tringa glareola
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。上體深褐色而有濃密的淡色斑點。眉紋白色，延伸至眼後。腳黃色。飛行時翼下顔色較淡，腰白色，尾白色及有褐色橫紋。見於任何水淹地區。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Winter Visitors. Dense pale spots on greyish brown upperparts, conspicuous white supercilium extends beyond the eyes, yellow legs. In flight, it shows pale underwings, white rump and white tail with brown bars. Found in any flooded area.
