黃腹鷦鶯
Yellow-bellied Prinia
Prinia flaviventris
無危。留鳥。小型鶯，體短尾長。頭灰色，眼前黃色眉紋延伸至眼上。上體褐色，腹部白色，脇較黃。叫聲像貓的「喵喵」聲。繁殖期有急促輕快的歌聲。見於草叢間出沒，但也會在其他有植物的生境出現。
Least Concern. Common Residents. Small-sized warbler with short body and long tail. Grey head with yellowish supercilium extending to top of eye. Brown upperparts. White underparts with yellowish flanks. Call like a cat-mew.also a rollicking song when breeding. Found in grassland but can occur in any vegetated habitat.
