黑枕燕鷗
Black-naped Tern
Sterna sumatrana
無危。夏候鳥。全身白色的中型燕鷗，明顯的黑色貫眼紋伸延至後枕，尾開叉成V形，嘴和腳黑色。幼鳥上體有褐色斑。見於於海上，在島嶼繁殖，有時靠近岸邊。
Least Concern. Summer Visitor. Medium-sized white tern. Prominent eyestripe extends to nape. Deeply forked tail, bill and legs black. The upperparts of juveniles heavily streaked brown. Marine and breeds on islands, sometimes close to shore.
