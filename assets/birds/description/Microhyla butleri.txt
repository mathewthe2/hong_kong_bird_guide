粗皮姬蛙
Butler's Pigmy Frog
Microhyla butleri
無危。細小的蛙類，三角形的身形。身長不超過3厘米。背部灰褐色，中央有深啡色，像聖誕樹的不規則形狀。背部有結節，吻尖突出。見於森林、溪流、農田、草地及池塘。
Least Concern. Small frog with triangular-shaped body. Total length less than 3cm. Back greyish-brown, the centre is marked with dark brown irregular shape which is like a christmas tree. Scattered tubercles over the back. Pointed snout. Found in forest, streams, cultivated fields, grassland and ponds.
