紅喉歌鴝
Siberian Rubythroat
Calliope calliope
無危。春及冬季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。全身大致棕色，體型壯碩。嘴深色，腳偏淡，上體褐色，有明顯白眉紋和頰紋。成年雄鳥喉部紅色，雌鳥喉部粉紅色。下體色較淺，第一年渡冬鳥像巨大的褐柳鶯。見於濃密下層叢林裏.
Least Concern. Spring and Winter Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Robust brown robin. Dark bill. Pale legs. Brown upperparts with prominent white eyebrow and moustachial stripe. Male has a distinctive red throat, which is pinkish on females. Paler underparts. First winter birds resemble robust Dusky Warblers.  Found in thick undergrowth.
