喜鵲
Oriental Magpie
Pica serica
無危。留鳥。大型鴉科鳥類。全身黑色，上身有藍色金屬光澤。肩及腹部白色。胸部、嘴和腳黑色。飛行時可見白色飛羽。見於開闊地區, 市區, 林地和濕地.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Large-sized bird. Black and white overall. Shiny metallic blue on black upperparts. White on shoulders and belly. Black on breast, bill and legs. White flight feathers can be see in flight. Found in open areas, urban areas, woodlands and wetlands.
