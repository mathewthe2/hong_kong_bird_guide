飾紋姬蛙
Ornate Pigmy Frog
Microhyla fissipes
無危。細小的蛙類，身長不超過3厘米。身體棕色，像扁平的三角形。背部有深褐色不規則花紋。大腿散佈褐色紋。見於森林、農田和池塘。
Least Concern. Small frog with maximum body length of 3cm. A brown and flat triangular-shaped body. With a long and irregular dark brown marking on the back. Scattered brown bands on hind legs. Found in forest, cultivated fields and ponds.
