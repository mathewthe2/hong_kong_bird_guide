三寶鳥
Oriental Dollarbird
Eurystomus orientalis
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥。嘴粗，紅色，嘴端有時沾黑，腳紅色。軀體藍綠色，頭部深色，喉部紫藍色。飛行時雙翼寬長，有明顯大白圓斑。見於樹木稀疏的山邊。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants. Bill red and thick, sometimes bill tip appears black. Red legs. Body is bluish green. Dark head with purplish blue throat. In flight, shows long broad wings with large round"dollar"-shaped white patches. Found in lightly wooded hillside.
