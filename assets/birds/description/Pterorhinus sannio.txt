白頰噪鶥
White-browed Laughingthrush
Pterorhinus sannio
無危。留鳥。全身褐色，和畫眉相似，眼眉和面頰均為白色。喜成小群出沒，叫聲類似黑臉噪鶥的「標－標－」聲，但有顫音。見於灌叢及耕地邊緣.
Least Concern. Resident. Brown body. Similar to Hwamei but with distinctive white eye brow and cheeks. Usually in small flocks. Call a loud “bill-bill-“ similar to Black-faced Laughing Thrush, but with quivering effect .Found in thickets and at edges of cultivation.
