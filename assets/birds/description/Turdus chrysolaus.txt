赤胸鶇
Brown-headed Thrush
Turdus chrysolaus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬侯鳥。頭部及喉部褐色，嘴基淡橙褐色，腳粉紅色。上身及尾部深褐色。胸及脇明顯橙色，腹部至尾下白色，尾下覆羽具有淡色斑紋。雌鳥及幼鳥眼眉淡褐色，喉部白色，具有幼細直紋。在曠地覓食，但通常離開植物掩蔽物不遠。
Least Conern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Brownish head and throat, bill base light brownish orange, pink legs. Upperparts and tail dark brown. Orange breast and flank, conspicuous white abdomen. Lower tail cover whitish, with pale streaks. Female and immature have pale eyebrow, whitish throat with narrow streaks.Feed in open but usually close to cover.
