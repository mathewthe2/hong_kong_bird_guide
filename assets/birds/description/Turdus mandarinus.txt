烏鶇
Chinese Blackbird
Turdus mandarinus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。上體黑色至深褐色，下體較淡。嘴由黃至褐色都有，眼圈可能不明顯，腳黑色。常小群出沒於樹上，鳴聲為哀怨的「dweep」. 見於林木疏落地區及市區公園地上覓食.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Winter Visitors. Black to brownish-black upperparts, underparts slightly paler. Bill varies from yellow to brownish, the eyering can be indistinct. Black legs. Often in flocks among trees. Call is a plaintive “dweep”. Found feeding in parks and open lightly woodland.
