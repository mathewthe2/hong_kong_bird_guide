灰背鶇
Grey-backed Thrush
Turdus hortulorum
無危。冬候鳥。嘴及腳淡色。雄鳥上體及胸為灰色，喉及腹白色，脇橙色。雌鳥及未成年鳥上體褐色，胸前有黑色斑紋。見於花園及林區, 很多時在寧靜的草地覓食.
Least Concern. Winter Visitors. Pale bill and legs. Male has distinctive grey upperparts and breast. Throat and belly are white. Flanks orange. Female and immature have brownish upperparts, black spots on breast and orange flanks.Found in garden and woodland, but feed in undisturbed lawns.
