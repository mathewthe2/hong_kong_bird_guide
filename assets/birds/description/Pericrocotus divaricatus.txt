灰山椒鳥
Ashy Minivet
Pericrocotus divaricatus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥。頭部黑色，前額、喉至面頰白色，嘴短黑色，上體深灰色，翼黑色，下體白色沾灰，腳黑色。幼鳥整體偏淡灰褐色。見於較開闊地區.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant. Black head. Forehead, throat and cheeks white. Short dark bill. Upperparts dark grey with black wings. White underparts with some grey. Black legs. Juvenile is pale greyish brown. Found in more open areas
