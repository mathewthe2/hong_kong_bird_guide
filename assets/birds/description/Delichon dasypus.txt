煙腹毛腳燕
Asian House Martin
Delichon dasypus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。上體深藍色，翼黑色，下體偏白。腳長有偏白色毛，腰白色，尾稍分叉。在山區和海岸聚居繁殖.
Least Concern.Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Winter Migrants. Upperparts deep blue with black wings. White underparts. Legs with whitish feathers. White rump. Tail slightly forked.Breeds in colonirs in mountains and seacoasts
