Mogannia Hebes
Mogannia hebes
體型較小。體色多樣，具綠色、黑色、黃褐色和綠褐色等。成蟲吸啜植物草的汁液維生。雄性以響亮的鳴叫爭奪異性。幼蟲孵化後會鑽入泥土內，直至羽化。
Smallest size cicada. Various colour, green, black, rufous-yellow and rufous green. Adults feed on sap of true grasses. Male adults use their loud call to compete female. Nymphs grow underground after hatching until emergence.
