黃腹山雀
Yellow-bellied Tit
Pardaliparus venustulus
無危。冬侯鳥及偶見鳥。體型細小的山雀。外型與常見的大山雀相似，但身型較小，尾較短。頭黑色，面頰白色，後枕上有一白斑。上背灰色,下體鮮黃色，中央沒有黑線。在林地繁殖,冬季集群在低窪林區和花園出現.
Least Concern. Winter Visitor, Occassional Visitor. Similar to Great Tit but smaller and shorter-tailed. Black head with white cheeks and a white spot on nape.Grey mantle. Bright yellow underparts without central black line. Breeding in montane woodland and occurring in flocks in lowlying woodlands and garden in winter.
