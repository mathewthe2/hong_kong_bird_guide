紅嘴藍鵲
Red-billed Blue Magpie
Urocissa erythroryncha
無危。留鳥。頭至胸部黑色，頭頂至後枕白色，嘴、虹膜和腳紅色。上體藍色，下體白色。有很長而帶藍色的尾羽，尾下羽毛黑色，末端有大白斑。常見於濃密灌叢內或周邊.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Black from head to breast, crown to nape white. Bill, iris and legs are red. Blue above and white below. Long blue tail tipped white. Found in near areas with thick shrubs
