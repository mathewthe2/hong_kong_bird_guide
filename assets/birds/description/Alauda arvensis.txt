雲雀
Eurasian Skylark
Alauda arvensis
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。大型雲雀。上身主要灰褐色，有深色斑點，眉紋偏黃色，面頰有一大片明顯的栗色。見於農地，草地和沼澤邊緣。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Large skylark. Upperparts greyish brown with dark stripes, prominent yellowish eyebrow and chestnut cheeks. Found in farmlands, grasslands and edge of marshes.
