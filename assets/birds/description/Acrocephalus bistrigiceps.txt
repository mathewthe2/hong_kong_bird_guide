黑眉葦鶯
Black-browed Reed Warbler
Acrocephalus bistrigiceps
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。全身濃棕色，沒有斑紋。眉紋白色，上有一道黑紋。飛行時腰部呈栗褐色。腹部白色，脇淡黃色。並不易見，歌聲與大葦鶯相似但較悅耳，見於水邊蘆葦和長草之間。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. A rich-brown warbler with no streaks. Distinctive black stripe above whitish supercilium. Rump slightly chestnut when flushed into flight. Underparts white with buff flanks. Not easy to see. Call like Oriental Reed Warbler but more melodious. Found reeds and long grass near water.
