青腳濱鷸
Temminck's Stint
Calidris temminckii
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。體型細小，羽色和磯鷸相似但較單調，腳黃綠色，頭、頸、胸和背有均勻的灰褐色，腹部白色。繁殖時背部灰褐色，羽毛邊緣黃褐色。見於淡水濕地。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Small, plain-looking wader resembling Common Sandpiper. Yellowish green legs. Greyish brown head, neck, breast and upperparts, white belly. In breeding plumage, upperparts greyish brown with brownish yellow fringes. Found in freshwater wetlands.
