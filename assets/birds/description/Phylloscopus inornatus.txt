黃眉柳鶯
Yellow-browed Warbler
Phylloscopus inornatus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。眼上有黃色長眉紋。上體淡橄欖綠色，三級飛羽上有白邊，翅膀上有兩道白色翼帶，腰淡黃色，下體污白色，腳粉紅色。叫聲為「tswe-et」。見於樹上高處.
Least Concern.Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Winter Visitors. Prominent yellowish long supercilium. Pale olive-green upperparts. White fringes on tertials. Two white wing bars. Pale yellow rump. Underparts dirty white. Pink legs. Call "tswe-et". Usually found in treetops.
