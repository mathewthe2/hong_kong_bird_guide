暗綠繡眼鳥
Swinhoe's White-eye
Zosterops simplex
無危。冬候鳥，留鳥。體型細小。頭、上身及尾部綠色，有明顯的白眼圈，嘴和腳黑色。喉和臀部黃色，胸和腹部白色。常成群一起活動，叫聲為輕柔的「tzee-tzee」聲。見於於林地
Least Concern. Winter Visitors, Common Residents. Small size. Head, upperparts and tail green, with prominent white eyering, black bill and legs. Throat and vent yellow, breast and belly white. Call a soft “tzee”. Wanders and feeds in flocks. Found in woodlands.
