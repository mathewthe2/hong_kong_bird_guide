淡腳柳鶯
Pale-legged Leaf Warbler
Phylloscopus tenellipes
無危。春及秋季遷徙過境鳥，冬候鳥。和庫頁島柳鶯外型十分相似。灰腳柳鶯一般體型較小。兩者上體皆為橄欖褐或橄欖綠色，眉紋長而闊，淺黃色，後方向上翹起。只有一道翼帶。上嘴邊緣淺色，令嘴尖看起來似有一淺色斑點。腳呈淡灰粉紅色。見於樹林下層。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Atlas. These two species are very similar. In hand, Pale-legged Leaf Warbler is usually smaller in size . Olive-brown or olive-green upperparts. Long broad pale yellowish supercilium with upward ‘kink’ in the rear.  One wing bar. Pale edge on upper mandible gives the illusion of a pale spot on bill tip. Legs pale grey-pink. Found in lower storey in wooded area.
