火冠雀
Fire-capped Tit
Cephalopyrus flammiceps
無危。迷鳥。亞種olivaceus於華西及中國西南部繁殖，東南亞越冬。小型林鳥，頭及上背綠色，雄性前額為紅色。
Least Concern. Vagrant. The subspecies olivaceus breeds at western and southwestern China and winters at Southeast Asia. It is a small woodland species which has a green head and mantle. Male birds have a red forehead.
