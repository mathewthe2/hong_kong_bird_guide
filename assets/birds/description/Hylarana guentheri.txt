沼蛙
Günther's Frog
Hylarana guentheri
無危。大型的蛙類，身長不超過10厘米。身體啡色或啡紅色。後肢有黑色橫斑。背部有兩條摺紋。見於溪流、農田和池塘。
Least Concern. Large frog with a maximum length of 10cm. Body brown or reddish brown. Black horizontal stripes on the hind limbs. Back with 2 dorsolateral folds. Found in streams, cultivated fields and ponds.
