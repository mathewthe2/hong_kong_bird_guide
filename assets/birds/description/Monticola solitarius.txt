藍磯鶇
Blue Rock Thrush
Monticola solitarius
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，夏候鳥及冬候鳥。嘴及腳黑色。pandoo亞種雄鳥全身閃亮藍色，而philippensis亞種則上體羽色相同，但腹部深棕色，兩種都有淡黑及近白色的鱗狀斑紋。雌鳥上體由深褐至灰藍都有，下體密佈黑色鱗狀斑紋。見於岩石上或屋頂。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Summer Visitor and Winter Visiter. Black bill and legs. Males range from all glossy blue (race pandoo) to glossy blue with a deep chestnut belly (race philippensis), with whitish and black scales on body. Females have dark brown to greyish blue upperparts with paler scaly underparts. Usually stands upright on exposed rocks or buildings.
