矛紋草鶥
Chinese Babax
Pterorhinus lanceolatus
無危。曾經在香港有野生種群的鳥種。棕色草鶥上身帶有灰色縱紋，白色下身和脇部帶長而棕色的縱紋。頭頂棕色帶有窄窄的灰色邊緣和有白色虹膜。嘈吵小群見於山區下層林叢。
Least Concern.Residents. Previously established feral species. Brown babax with grey streaks above. White with long brown flank streaks below. Brown Crown with narrow grey edging and white iris. Small noisy parties in montane undergrowth.
