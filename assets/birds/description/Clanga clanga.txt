烏鵰
Greater Spotted Eagle
Clanga clanga
易危。冬候鳥。大型深褐色猛禽，爪和嘴黃色。幼鳥全身有白色斑點。飛行時，比白肩鵰翅膀較寬，尾部較短圓。飛羽不時有殘缺。見於后海灣一帶濕地上空。
Vulnerable. Winter visitors. Large raptor in brown with yellow bill and claws in adult. Juvenile has white spots. Has broader wings and shorter rounded tail in flight than Imperial Eagle. Sometimes seen with broken feathers. Found above the wetlands in the Deep Bay area.
