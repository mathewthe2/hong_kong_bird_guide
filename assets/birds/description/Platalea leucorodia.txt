白琵鷺
Eurasian Spoonbill
Platalea leucorodia
無危。冬候鳥。有琵琶形狀的嘴，全身白色，腳和腳指黑色，嘴黑色，未成年鳥嘴粉紅色。見於后海灣一帶泥灘丶魚塘丶基圍，和河道。
Least Concern. Winter Visitors. Long spoon-like bill. Mainly in White. Legs and feet are black. Pink bill in immatures are dark in adults. Seen in mudflats, river channels, fish ponds and gei wais around the Deep Bay area.
