小棘蛙
Lesser Spiny Frog
Quasipaa exilispinosa
易危。中型的蛙類，身長8至10厘米。皮膚粗糙並帶有小瘤粒。 背部灰色或啡紅色，並有十分不明顯的斑點及頗多皺紋。虹膜呈交叉狀。趾間帶全蹼。見於溪流。
Vulnerable. Medium-sized hill stream frog with a flat body with length of 8-10cm. Rough skin with tubercules and granules. Greyish-brown or reddish-brown back with dark indistinct mottling and numerous wrinkles. Toes entirely webbed. Found in streams.
