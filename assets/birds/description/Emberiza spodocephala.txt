灰頭鵐
Black-faced Bunting
Emberiza spodocephala
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。頭部深灰綠色，上體褐色，有濃密深色縱紋；下體淡黃色，有褐色縱紋。香港有幾個亞種，最常見的是personata亞種，有淡白色頰下紋，胸前有灰褐色縱紋。常見於林邊田野。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Winter Visitors. Head greenish grey, upperparts brown with heavy dark streaks, underparts yellow with brownish stripes. There are several sub-species. The most common, personata has a pale submoustachial stripe and greenish grey stripes on the breast.Found in field aside woodland.
