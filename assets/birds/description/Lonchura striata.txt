白腰文鳥
White-rumped Munia
Lonchura striata
無危。留鳥。嘴部黑色，呈圓椎形，前額、眼先和喉部黑色。頭、上體和胸部深褐色，腰部和腹部明顯明白色。尾羽和飛羽黑色。常成群活動。見於於田野長草堆中.
Least Concern. Resident. Large, black conical bill. Black forehead, lores and throat. Head, upperparts and breast dark brown, with prominent white rump and belly. Black tail and flight feathers. Often in small flocks.Found in long grasses in filed.
