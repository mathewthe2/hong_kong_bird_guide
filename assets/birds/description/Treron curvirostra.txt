厚嘴綠鳩
Thick-billed Green Pigeon
Treron curvirostra
無危。偶見鳥。淡綠色嘴厚，嘴基紅色和明顯的灰藍色眼眶是最主要的特徴。雄性背部及覆羽紫紅色，尾下覆羽栗色。 雌鳥和幼鳥綠色為主。
Least Concern. Occasional Visitor. Thick green bill with red at base and and prominent grey-blue orbital skin are the main characteristics to separate from similar Green Pigeons. Male has maroon mantle and cinnamon under tail coverts. Female and juveniles are mainly green.
