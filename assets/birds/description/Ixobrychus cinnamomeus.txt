栗葦鳽
Cinnamon Bittern
Ixobrychus cinnamomeus
無危。春、秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬、夏候鳥。小型鷺鳥。成鳥上體和翼均勻紅褐色，可和其他鳽鳥區別。雄鳥羽色較鮮明，雌鳥上體較暗，下體縱紋較多。幼鳥和池鷺相似，但褐色羽毛上有粗縱紋和黑白斑點。見於濕地，特別是蘆葦叢。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Summer and Winter Visitors. Small ardeid with uniform reddish brown upperparts and wings, which helps separate it from other bitterns. Female is duller with more streaking below.  Juvenile thickly-striped with light brown spots on brown feathers. Found in the wetlands, especially in reed beds.
