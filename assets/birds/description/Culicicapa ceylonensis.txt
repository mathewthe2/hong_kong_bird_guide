方尾鶲
Grey-headed Canary-flycatcher
Culicicapa ceylonensis
無危。冬候鳥。頭和胸灰色，上體及翼橄欖綠色，下體黃色。叫聲為獨特的「silly billy」。偏好樹林, 在高地林區繁殖, 冬季移到海拔較低處.
Least Concern. Winter Visitors. Grey head and breast. Olive green upperparts and wings, and yellow underparts. Distinctive four-note call "silly billy". Favours woodland, breeds in upland forests and moves to lower elevations in winter
