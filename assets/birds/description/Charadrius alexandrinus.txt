環頸鴴
Kentish Plover
Charadrius alexandrinus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。腳深色，嘴黑色。胸帶在胸前斷開，與金眶鴴有別。繁殖羽後額有黑色斑，頭頂至後枕栗色，胸帶黑色，在胸前及背後斷開。見於后海灣一帶泥灘和開闊地。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Dark legs and black bill. Broken breast band distinguishes it from Little Ringed Plover. Breeding plumage shows black patch behind forehead, chestnut crown and nape, black breastband broken at the breast and hindneck. Found in mudflats and open areas around the Deep Bay area.
