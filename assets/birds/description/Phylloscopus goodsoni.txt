古氏[冠紋]柳鶯
Hartert's Leaf Warbler
Phylloscopus goodsoni
無危。冬候鳥。大型而有冠紋的柳鶯。有明顯黃色冠紋，上體呈綠色，下體白色。側冠紋偏黑至深綠色，有黃色闊眉紋。和相似的冕柳鶯比較，臀部沒有黃色。見於森林的中及上層活動。
Least Concern. Winter Visitor. A large ‘crowned’ warbler. Prominent yellow crown stripe, greenish upperparts with white underparts. Blackish to dark green lateral crown stripes. Broad yellowish supercilium. Compared to similar Eastern Crown Warbler, Blyth’s Leaf Warbler has no yellow on vent. Favours high and middle storeys in woodland.
