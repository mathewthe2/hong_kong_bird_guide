金頭扇尾鶯
Golden-headed Cisticola
Cisticola exilis
無危。冬候鳥。看似鷦鶯，具有長尾，以及明顯淡灰色耳羽和白喉。上身褐色，頭部深灰色及明顯白色條紋。上背、翼及尾羽深褐色。下體淡黃褐色。見於長有長草的開闊原野。
Least Concern. Winter Visitor. Resembles a prinia, with long tail. Greyish-white ear coverts and white throat. Upperparts brownish, head darkish with narrow light stripes. Wings to tail dark brown. Underparts light brownish-yellow. Monosyllabic nasal call “tzee”. Found in open country with long grasses.
