大擬啄木鳥
Great Barbet
Psilopogon virens
無危。留鳥。嘴粗，黃色，腳淡色。頭部深色，上背深褐色，翼、下背至尾部綠色，下體淡黃，有深褐色粗縱紋，尾下覆羽紅色。常在春天鳴叫，叫聲像噪鵑，但較為哀怨，不斷重覆。見於於林地.
Least Concern. Resident. Bill yellow and thick, pale legs. Dark head, upperparts dark brown. Wings and underparts to tail green, underparts pale yellow with dark brown thick streaks. Undertail coverts red. Call like Koel but more plaintive, continuously repeated in spring. Found in woodlands.
