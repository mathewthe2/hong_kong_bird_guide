黑領椋鳥
Black-collared Starling
Gracupica nigricollis
無危。留鳥。大型椋鳥，頭部白色，眼部周圍皮膚黃色。有明顯黑色領帶，上體和翼深褐或黑色，羽毛邊緣白色。嘴黑色，腳淡粉紅色。幼鳥沒有黑色領帶，頭部褐色。叫聲響亮愉快的"chee-we-chee chee-we-chee". 見於任何開闊低地.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Large starling. White head, with yellow bare skin around the eye. Prominent black collar, upperparts and wings dark brown or black with white tips. Juvenile has no black collar and is browner overall. Call is loud cheerful piping cry "chee-we-chee chee-we-chee". Found in any open lowland areas.
