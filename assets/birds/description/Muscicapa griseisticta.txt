灰紋鶲
Grey-streaked Flycatcher
Muscicapa griseisticta
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥。大致灰褐色，翅膀長及尾部的一半。淺色眼先，白喉上有鮮明顎紋。胸部有明顯的褐色條紋，和白色腹部成強烈對比。翼上有白邊。見於沒有遮閉的樹枝上。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passsage Migrant. A greyish-brown Flycatcher. Long wings reach up to half of tail. Pale lores. Strong malar stripes on white throat. Prominent brown streaks on breast contrast with white belly. White edges on wing panels. Perches on exposed branches.
