斑文鳥
Scaly-breasted Munia
Lonchura punctulata
無危。留鳥。嘴部黑色，呈圓椎形，眼先和喉部深褐色。頭、上體和胸部褐色，下體白色，胸和脇的羽毛邊緣褐色像鱗片，尾羽和飛羽褐色。常成群活動，有時與其他文鳥混在一起。叫聲為輕柔而重複的「mit-mit…」聲。見於草地和荒廢地的長草堆中.
Least Concern. Resident. Large, black conical bill. Dark brown lores and throat. Head, upperparts and breast brown, underparts white, feathers at the breast and flanks fringed brown and appear scaly. Brown tail and flight feathers. Often in groups, sometimes mixes with other munias. The call is a soft “mit-mit…”. Found in long grasses in grasslands and abandoned lands.
