反嘴鷸
Pied Avocet
Recurvirostra avosetta
無危。冬候鳥。黑白兩色為主。有細長的黑嘴，嘴的末端向上彎。頭頂至後頸、翼上覆羽、翼角和翼尖都為黑色。見於泥灘和河道。
Least Concern. Winter Visitors. Black-and-white Avocet has a narrow, long, up-curved bill. Black from crown to hindneck, on wing tips and carpal joints, as well as on uppering coverts. Found in mudflats and river channels.
