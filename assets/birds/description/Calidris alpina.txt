黑腹濱鷸
Dunlin
Calidris alpina
無危。春及秋季過境遷徒鳥，冬候鳥。中型水鳥，黑色末端稍向下彎。與彎嘴濱鷸相比，腳較短、嘴較直。繁殖期背部沾紅棕色，腹部明顯黑色。非繁殖期上體為單調的灰褐色，頸部有細縱紋，下體白色。見於潮澗帶泥灘和魚塘間的基堤。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Medium–sized wader. Bill tip slightly decurved. Compared with Curlew Sandpiper, has shorter legs and straighter bill. In breeding plumage, upperparts rufous brown, with conspicuous big black belly. Non-breeding plumage is dull greyish brown on the upperparts, with thin dark stripes on the neck. Underparts white. Found in tidal mudflats and fishpond bunds.
