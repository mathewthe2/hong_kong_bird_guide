小白鷺
Little Egret
Egretta garzetta
無危。留鳥。全身白色，體型小。嘴和腳黑色，腳趾黃色。繁殖期時頭上有兩條細長飾羽，面頰裸露皮膚變黃綠色，甚至帶有紅色。見於各類型濕地。
Least Concern. Common residents. Medium to small pure white egrets. Bill and legs are black and toes are yellow. In breeding season, two narrow elongated nape feathers appear behind the head and yellowish green facial skin flushes bright red. Found in various wetlands.
