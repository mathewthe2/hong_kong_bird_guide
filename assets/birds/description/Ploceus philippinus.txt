黃胸織雀
Baya Weaver
Ploceus philippinus
無危。曾經在香港有野生種群的鳥種。嘴黑而粗厚，全　身黃褐色，上體有深色縱紋。繁殖期雄鳥面頰黑色，頭頂和後枕鮮黃色。雌鳥顏色較淡。
Least Concern.  Previously established feral species. Bill thick and black. Overall plumage brownish yellow, with dark streaks on upperparts. Breeding male has prominent black cheeks and bright yellow crown and nape. Female is paler in colour.
