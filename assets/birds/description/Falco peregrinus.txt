Peregrine Falcon
Falco peregrinus
無危。冬候鳥及留鳥。中型猛禽，軀體粗壯。成鳥上體灰色，下體白色並有深色細橫紋，臉部有深色粗頰紋。幼鳥偏褐色，胸部有褐色縱紋。見於任何開闊原野和市區.
Least Concern. Winter Visitor and Resident. Medium-sized raptor. Stout body. Adult has grey upperparts, white belly with dark narrow stripes and a dark, thick moustachial stripe. Juvenile is brownish in color with brown streaks on belly. Found in any open areas and urban areas
