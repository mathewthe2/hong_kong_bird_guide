髮冠卷尾
Hair-crested Drongo
Dicrurus hottentottus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，留鳥。頭和嘴部大而粗厚，嘴後的細長羽毛伸延至頭後面。全身黑色，在陽光下可見到閃亮的銅輝。尾部闊大，沒有開叉，末端向兩旁翹起。叫聲吵啞剌耳。見於林區及公園.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Resident. Head and bill look heavy. Elongated crest trails backward from behind the bill. Shiny black overall, with a copper-green sheen under sunlight. Broad tail feathers upturned at the ends but not forked.. Rasping call.Found in woodland and parks .
