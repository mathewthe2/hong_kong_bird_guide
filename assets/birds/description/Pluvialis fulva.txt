太平洋金斑鴴
Pacific Golden Plover
Pluvialis fulva
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。繁殖期時上體有金黃色、黑白斑點，與黑色下體之間以粗白線分隔。非繁殖期時及幼鳥的下體羽色變淡，沒有粗白線。見於后海灣泥灘。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Winter Visitors. Gold-spangled upperparts with black and white spots on breeding birds, black underparts marked out by a broad white line. In non-breeding birds, white line and black underparts are absent. Found in mudflats around the Deep Bay area.
