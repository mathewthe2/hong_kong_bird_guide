盧氏小樹蛙
Romer's Tree Frog
Liuixalus romeri
瀕危。細小的蛙類。雄性平均身長1.5厘米，雌性可達2.5厘米。為香港的特有種，也是世界上其中一種最細小的兩棲動物。背部呈棕色，帶有一個"X"形斑紋。兩眼之間有一道黑帶紋。足趾有小吸盤。見於溪流和草地。
Endangered. Tiny frog with an average of 1.5cm for males and 2.5 for females. An endemic species of Hong Kong and one of the world's tiniest amphibians. Brown back with an "X"-like marking. All digits have small suction discs. Inhabit in streams and grassland.
