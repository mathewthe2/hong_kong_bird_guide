懷氏地鶇
White's Thrush
Zoothera aurea
無危。冬候鳥。嘴及腳淡黃色，較其他鶇大，全身金褐色而有黑色鱗狀斑紋，腹部斑紋較稀疏。尾外緣有明顯的白點。常單獨出現。見於林區和較開闊地區.
Least Concern. Winter Visitors. Pale yellow bill and legs. Large and striking golden-brown thrush with distinctive black crescent-shaped markings above and below. The markings are less heavy on the belly. Conspicuous white tips to dark outer tail feathers. Usually solitary.Found in both wooded and more open areas.
