褐翅鴉鵑
Greater Coucal
Centropus sinensis
無危。留鳥。體型大，成鳥全身黑色而帶有光澤，翅膀鮮明栗色，嘴黑色，虹膜鮮紅色。幼鳥全身帶橫紋。常躲在樹叢下層，甚少飛行。發出低沉的「胡—胡—」聲。
Least Concern. Common Residents. Large adult has glossy black body with bright chestnut wings. Black bill and bright red iris. Juveniles are barred all over. Usually stay at under cover inside bushes. Call is a repeated low pitched "woo woo " sound.
