花姬蛙
Marbled Pigmy Frog
Microhyla pulchra
無危。細小的蛙類，身長不超過5厘米。身體三角形。乳脂色的背部有一個反轉的V狀條紋。兩眼間有一條深色帶紋。見於森林、農地和草地。
Least Concern. Small frog with maximum length of 5cm. Triangularly-shaped body. Back creamy with inverted "V" stripes. With a dark band between the eyes. Found in forest, cultivated fields and grassland.
