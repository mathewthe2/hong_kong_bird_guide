樹鷚
Olive-backed Pipit
Anthus hodgsoni
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。上體橄欖綠色 ，下體較淡，胸前及脇有濃密深色縱紋，眉紋明顯，耳羽後有白點，上嘴深色，下嘴粉紅色，腳淡紅色。常上下擺尾，受驚時會飛到電線或樹枝上。聲音為輕柔的「tseep」聲。見於耕地和林區.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Olive above. Underparts pale, heavy dark streaks on breast and flanks. Prominent eyebrow and spot behind ear coverts, upper bill dark, lower bill pale pink. Wags tail up and down. When flushed, it flies up to wires or bare branches. Call a light and soft “tseep”. Found in cultivation lands and woodland
