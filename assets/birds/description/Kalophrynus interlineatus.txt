花細狹口蛙
Spotted Narrow-mouthed Frog
Kalophrynus interlineatus
無危。細小的蛙類，身形像蟾蜍。身長可達6厘米。背部褐黃色並有多條深褐色縱虛線，而且有很多小疣粒。見於森林、農田及草地。
Least Concern. Small frog with toad-like body shape. Grow up to 6cm. Back yellowish-brown with many dark brown vertical dashed stripes and a large number of granules. Found in forest, cultivated fields and grassland.
