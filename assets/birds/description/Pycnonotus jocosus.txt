紅耳鵯
Red-whiskered Bulbul
Pycnonotus jocosus
無危。留鳥。全身偏褐，頭、嘴和腳黑色，有獨特直立冠羽，耳羽紅色，面頰及喉白色，有明顯黑色頰紋。下體淡褐色，臀部橙紅色。常發出清脆的"咇標咇" 歌聲.見於公園.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Dark brown upperparts. Black head, bill and legs, prominent erect crown feather. Red ear coverts, white cheeks and throat, with black moustachial stripe. Brown upperparts to tail, which is tipped with white. Underparts pale brown with orange red vent.Often sing a clear 'bill-bu-bill" song. Found in parks.
