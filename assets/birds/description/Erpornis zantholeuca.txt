白腹鳳鶥
White-bellied Erpornis
Erpornis zantholeuca
無危。留鳥。外型頗似鶯類，但可憑短冠羽和黃色尾下覆羽區別。頭頂、背部至尾部綠色，喉至腹部灰白色。常混在其他鳥群中出沒，叫聲為「tsee-tsee-tsee-tsee」。見於樹林。
Least Concern. Resident. Resembles a warbler, but distinguished by short crest and yellow vent. Crown, mantle to tail are green. Throat to belly greyish-white. Often in mixed flocks. Calls are “tsee-tsee-tsee-tsee”. Found in forest.
