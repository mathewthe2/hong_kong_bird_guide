赤麂
Red Muntjac
Muntiacus muntjak
無危。鹿科動物。栗棕色上身和白色下身。雄性有鹿角，但每年會脫落和重長，雌性沒有角。尾短，四肢長。棲息於多斜坡的灌木草原和刺灌木林。食物以嫩枝葉和果實為主。
Least Concern. deer-like animals. chesnut brown above and white below. Males have antlers that shed and regrow annually while female do not have. Short tail and long limbs. Inhabit in shrub grassland and thorny shrubland on hilly ranges. Feed on tender leaves,twigs and fruits.
