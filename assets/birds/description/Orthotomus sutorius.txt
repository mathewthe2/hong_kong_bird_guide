長尾縫葉鶯
Common Tailorbird
Orthotomus sutorius
無危。留鳥。小型鶯, 尾長嘴長，前額和頭頂紅褐色，背部至尾部橄欖綠色，下體白色，喉部有時可見到黑紋。叫聲是「即—即—」聲。廣泛分佈於林地.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Small warbler with long bill and tail, with a reddish-brown forehead and crown. Olive-green mantle to tail. White underparts. Throat may show some blackish streaks. Call a distinctive loud and repeating "chink-chink-chink".Found in woodlands.
