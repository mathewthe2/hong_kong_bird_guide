蒙古沙鴴
Lesser Sand Plover
Charadrius mongolus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。體型較大的鴴。嘴短而纖細；腳偏綠，可和鐵嘴沙鴴區別。見於后海灣一帶各類型濕地。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Large plover. Compared with Greater Sand Plover, it has shorter, finer bill and greenish legs. A thin black line separates breast band and white throat. Found in various wetland habitats around the Deep Bay area.
