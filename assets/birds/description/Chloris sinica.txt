金翅雀
Grey-capped Greenfinch
Chloris sinica
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候烏，留鳥。頭部灰黃色，上體深黃褐色，飛羽黑色，有顯眼黃斑，尾羽黑色。喉至下體黃褐色，尾下覆羽黃色。嘴和腳淡粉紅色。雌鳥羽色較淡。見於開闊原野及近郊.
least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor, Resident. Head yellowish grey, upperparts dark yellowish brown, flight feathers black with distinctive yellow marks, tail black. Throat to underparts yellowish-brown, undertail coverts yellow. Bill and legs pale pink. Female is paler. Found in open country and suburban areas.
