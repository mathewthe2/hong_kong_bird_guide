紅領綠鸚鵡
Rose-ringed Parakeet
Psittacula krameri
無危。留鳥。外地引入品種，全身綠色，嘴紅色，眼圈紅色。雄鳥頸部有粉紅色細領環，環上有黑邊；頸側至後頸沾藍，尾部藍色。展翅滑翔時狀似「十」字，見於市區內大型公園。
Least Concern. Resident. Introduced green parakeet, with red bill and eyering. Male has thin pink collar with black fringe. Side and hind neck bluish, long tail blue in colour. Cross-shaped profile in flight. Found in large urban parks.
