黑眉雀鶥
Huet's Fulvetta
Alcippe hueti
無危。偶見鳥。頭部灰色，有明顯白眼圈，嘴黑色。上體至尾部褐色，喉及下體淡黃褐色，沾有灰斑，腳粉紅色。見於下層林叢林和樹林中層集群.
Least Concern. Occasional Visitor. Small forest babbler. Greyish head with prominent white eyering. Black bill. Upperparts to tail brown. Throat to underparts pale yellowish brown, with grey patches. Pink legs. Flocks found in undergrowth and mid-storey.
