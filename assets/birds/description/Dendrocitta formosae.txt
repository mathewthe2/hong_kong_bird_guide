灰樹鵲
Grey Treepie
Dendrocitta formosae
無危。冬候鳥，留鳥。尾長而嘴粗，整體深色而尾部黑色，背部栗色。叫聲嘈吵而帶機器聲音。見於樹林中或周邊成群出現.
Least Concern. Winter Visitor, Common Residents. Long tail and thick bill. Dull-coloured body with long black tail and chestnut mantle. Noisy, with a variety of mechanical-sounding calls. Often found in flocks in or near woodlands.
