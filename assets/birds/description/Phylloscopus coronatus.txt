冕柳鶯
Eastern Crowned Warbler
Phylloscopus coronatus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥。上體鮮明黃橄欖色，有幼長淡色冠紋和眉紋，眼先和過眼線黑色，飛羽有淡色羽緣，有一條黃色翼帶。下體白色，臀部淡黃色。見於開闊林區。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant. Bright yellowish-olive upperparts. Long and narrow pale crown strip and supercilium. Dark lore and eye-stripe. Flight feathers has plaefrings with one yellow wing bar. White underparts and yellow vent.Found in open woodland
