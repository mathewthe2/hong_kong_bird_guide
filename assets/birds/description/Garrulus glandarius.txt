松鴉
Eurasian Jay
Garrulus glandarius
無危。留鳥。全身淡褐色。虹膜白色，有黑色眼圈。嘴黑色，有粗黑頰紋。飛羽和尾羽黑色。翼上有明顯藍白黑三色色斑，腰白色。見於在任何林地和樹林.
Least Concern. Resident. Overall pale pink plumage. White iris, black eyerings. Black bill with thick black submoustachial stripes. Flight feathers and tail black, with distinctive blue, black and white patches on wings. White rump. Found woodland and forest of all kinds.
