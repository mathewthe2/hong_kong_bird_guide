黑眉柳鶯
Sulphur-breasted Warbler
Phylloscopus ricketti
無危。迷鳥。頭黑黃相間，十分搶眼。冠紋和眉紋黃色，側冠紋和過眼線黑綠色，眼圈不明顯，後頸有灰色細紋，上嘴深色，下嘴偏黃。上體綠色，下體鮮明黃色。翼上有兩度翼斑。很多時在林區覓食.
Least Concern. Vagrant. Eyecatching black-and-yellow stripes on head. Crown stripe and supercilium yellow. Lateral crown stripe and eye-stripe darkish green. Eye-ring not prominent. Fine grayish streaks at hind neck. Upper bill dark colour, lower bill yellowish. Green upperparts and bright yellow underparts. Two wing bars on flight feathers. Mostly feed in woodland.
