黑眶蟾蜍
Asian Common Toad
Duttaphrynus melanostictus
無危。身體褐黃色。平均身長可達6厘米，最大可長至10厘米。頭部三角形，有黑色的眼眶和指尖。眼後有腺體，能夠分泌奶白色毒液。手指之間沒有蹼。見於森林、灌木林，農田及草地。
Least Concern. Yellowish-brown body. Average length 6cm but some can grow up to 10 cm. Triangular head with dark ridges and finger tip. Glands behind the eyes can secrete milky toxic fluid. Fingers and toes are webless. Found in forests, shrubland, cultivated fields and grassland.
