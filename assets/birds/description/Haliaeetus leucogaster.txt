白腹海鵰
White-bellied Sea Eagle
Haliaeetus leucogaster
無危。留鳥。大型猛禽，飛行時黑白分明，容易辨認。成鳥頭、頸至下體及尾羽白色，尾楔形。滑翔時雙翼稍為擧起成Ｖ形，間中停棲在樹上，靜立時上體灰白色。見於沿岸水域。
Least Concern. Resident. Large black-and-white raptor, unmistakable in flight. Adult has white head, neck, underparts and tail. Wedge-shaped tail. V-shaped wings when gliding. Light grey upperparts when perched. Found in coastal waters.
