灰背椋鳥
White-shouldered Starling
Sturnia sinensis
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，夏、冬候鳥。頭部灰色，有白眼圈，嘴和腳灰色。上體灰色，肩部有一片大白斑，飛羽黑色，腰白色，下體白色。見於開闊地區, 間中在市區.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Summer and Winter Visitors. Grey head with white iris. Bill and legs grey. Upperparts grey, with prominent white scapulars. Flight feathers black, rump white. Underparts white.Found in open areas and occasionally in urban areas.
