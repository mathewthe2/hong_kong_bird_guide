八哥
Crested Myna
Acridotheres cristatellus
無危。留鳥。全身黑色而有光澤，虹膜橙黃色，嘴的上部和頭頂之間有長冠羽。嘴黃色，腳淡粉紅色。飛行時，翼底有明顯大白斑；靜止或站立時，兩旁有兩片白斑，如倒轉的「八」字。見於任何開闊地區和市區.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Shiny black, with yellow-orange iris and long tufts above the bill. Bill yellow, legs pale pink. A distinctive white patch under each wing in flight. Two small white marks on the folded wing at rest. Found in any open areas and urban areas .
