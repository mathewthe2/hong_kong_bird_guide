黑枕王鶲
Black-naped Monarch
Hypothymis azurea
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。頭部和嘴藍色，眼深色，尾下覆羽白色，站立時身體靠近水平方式橫置。雄鳥全身藍色，枕部有黑色斑塊，前頸有一道幼黑色領紋。雌鳥上體至尾部灰褐色，腹部偏白，帶有藍和褐色。見於樹林下層和林地邊緣。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Head and bill bright blue, dark eyes, white undertail coverts. Horizontal posture. Male is overall blue in plumage, with a black patch at nape and a narrow black collar in front neck. Female has greyish brown upperparts to tail, white belly tint with blue and brown. Found in lower forest or forest edge.
