尖尾濱鷸
Sharp-tailed Sandpiper
Calidris acuminata
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥。頭頂深栗色，與淡色眼眉成對比；上體羽毛深色，與淺色羽緣對比鮮明。嘴短而微向下彎。繁殖羽胸部銹褐色；上體為鮮鐵銹色。非繁殖羽顏色較暗淡。幼鳥顏色鮮明，胸部沾橙色。見於淡水沼澤和潮澗帶濕地。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant. Dark rufous crown contrasts with pale eyebrow. Dark upperparts with pale fringes. Bill short and tip slightly decurved. In breeding plumage, chest rusty, upperparts orange. Non-breeding birds are duller.  Juveniles are brightly coloured, with orange on the chest.
