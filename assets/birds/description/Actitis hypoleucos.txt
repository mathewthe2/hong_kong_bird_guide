磯鷸
Common Sandpiper
Actitis hypoleucos
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，夏候鳥。褐色上體，淡色顏眉，黃腳。站立時翼前有彎月形白斑，飛行時有明顯白色翼帶。見於岸邊、水道，以及魚塘和水塘邊緣。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrants, Summer Visitors. Brownish upperparts with pale supercilium and yellow legs. Prominent white crescents shown in front of the wings at rest. White wing bars can be seen in flight. Occurs along the coast, by watercourses and on banks of fishponds and reservoirs.
