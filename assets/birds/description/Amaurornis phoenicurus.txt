白胸苦惡鳥
White-breasted Waterhen
Amaurornis phoenicurus
無危。留鳥。香港最常見秧雞。臉至上腹白色，與深灰褐色背部成鮮明對比。嘴部青黃色，上嘴基紅色。腳淡黃色。發出像普通話「苦惡、苦惡」的叫聲。見於水邊的田地或草地。
Least Concern. Common Residents. Commonest rail in Hong Kong. Face to upper belly in white, contrasting with the dull brownish grey upperparts. Bill greenish yellow, with base of upper bill red. Legs pale yellow. Loud "Kuo-oa,Kuo-oa" call. Found in farmlands or grasslands aside waters.
