東方大葦鶯
Oriental Reed Warbler
Acrocephalus orientalis
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。香港大型葦鶯。上身淺褐色，眉紋明顯淡黃色，腹部白色， 脇薑褐色。嘴長黑色，尾長，尾端毛色較淡。會發出一連串沙啞的‘charh-charh-charh’叫聲，見於草地及沼澤活動，但也會在樹冠下出現。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Large warbler. Pale brown upperparts with prominent yellowish supercilium. Bill black and long. Underparts whitish with gingery brown on flanks. Tail long with pale tips. Call is a series of dry ‘charh-charh-charh’. Faound in grassy and marshy areas, but also can appear under canopy.
