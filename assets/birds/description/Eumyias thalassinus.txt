銅藍鶲
Verditer Flycatcher
Eumyias thalassinus
無危。冬候鳥。全身呈閃亮藍綠色，尾下覆羽末端白色。雄鳥有黑色眼先，雌鳥眼先較暗且較纖細。在林地出現, 見於明顯易見的地方。
Least Concern. Winter Visitor. Glossy bluish-green body. White tips on undertail coverts. Male has black lore. Female is duller and has smaller black lore area. Found in woodlands, Perches prominently.
