大草鶯
Chinese Grassbird
Graminicola striatus
易危。留鳥。全身大致褐色，頭圓，嘴粗壯，尾長而翼短，看起來有如一隻體形健碩的鷦鶯。後頸羽毛有黑白條紋，腹部白色，脇及尾下覆羽淡黃色。見於高山草地，但冬天時也會降到低地棲息。
Vulnerable. Resident. Mainly brown. Round head, stout bill, short wings with long tail, resembling a robust prinia. Black-and-white streaks on nape. Whitish underparts with buff flanks and vent.Found in grassland at high altitude, descends to lower levels in winter.
