白鶺鴒
White Wagtail
Motacilla alba
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，留鳥,冬候鳥。黑白灰三色，尾長，冬羽顏色較暗。羽色多變。leucopsis亞種（最常見）：臉部全白，無貫眼紋。見於開闊地區,河道附近.
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor, resident. Plumage consists of black, white and grey. Long-tailed. Grey upperparts more prominent in winter. Variable plumage colors. Race leucopsis (commonest): complete white face with no eyestripe.  Found in open areas or around river channels.
