黃雀
Eurasian Siskin
Spinus spinus
無危。冬候鳥。小型雀科，主要顏色有黃、綠和黑。雌雄均有兩道明顯黃色翼斑。間中出現的冬候鳥，見於樹上,大群可於馬尾松的頂部出現。
Least Concern. Winter Visitor. Tiny finch, mainly yellow, green and black in colour. Both sexes have two bright yellow wing-bars on a dark flying feathers. Irruptive winter visitor, arboreal,can occur in large flocks favouring the tops of trees such as Casuarina equisetifolia and Liquidambar formosana.
