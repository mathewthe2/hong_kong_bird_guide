中華攀雀
Chinese Penduline Tit
Remiz consobrinus
無危。春及秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。外貌有如一隻細小的伯勞。繁殖時雄鳥頭灰色，有黑色貫眼紋，後頸有偏紅色斑。雌鳥顏色偏褐。叫聲獨特，為音調較高的「tsee-tsee」聲。多成小群，但偶爾也會大群出沒。見於蘆葦叢中。
Least Concern. Spring and Autumn Passage Migrant. Like a tiny shrike. Breeding male has grey head, black eye stripe and reddish patch on hindneck. Female is browner. A distinctive high-pitched ‘tsee-tsee’ call. Usually in flocks, sometimes in large ones. Found in reedbeds.
