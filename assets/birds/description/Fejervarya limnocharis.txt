澤蛙
Paddy Frog/terrestrial Frog
Fejervarya limnocharis
無危。中等身型的蛙類，身長不超過6.5厘米。背部啡或啡綠色，有棒狀膚褶。上下唇有深色縱紋。有些個體有背中線。見於溪流、農田和池塘。
Least Concern. Medium-sized frog with a maximum legnth of 6.5cm. Back brown or greenish brown with raised wrinkles. Snout with dark vertical bands. Some individuals have a mid-dorsal stripe. Found in streams, cultivated fields and ponds.
