黑喉噪鶥
Black-throated Laughingthrush
Pterorhinus chinensis
無危。留鳥。深色的中型噪鶥，上體灰黑色，下體較淺色。臉及喉黑色，面頰是鮮明的白色。常小群出現，鳴聲嘹亮，如口哨般「胡壺─胡壺─」，有時會模仿其他雀鳥的叫聲。見於濃密下層林叢裏.
Least Concern. Common Residents. A dark laughingthrush. Dark grey upperparts and paler underparts. Black face and throat. Prominent white cheeks. Usually in small flocks. Loud whistling call, like musical note “do – sol”. Imitates other bird calls. Found in thick undergrowth.
