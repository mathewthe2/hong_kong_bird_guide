黃腰柳鶯
Pallas's Leaf Warbler
Phylloscopus proregulus
無危。秋季過境遷徙鳥，冬候鳥。香港體型最小的柳鶯。上體偏綠，腰部顯眼黃色，有淺黃色的冠紋和深綠色的側冠紋，眉長而呈黃色。翅膀上有兩道白色翼帶，下體污白色。見於樹林的中上層活動。
Least Concern. Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Smallest phylloscopus warbler in Hong Kong. Greenish upperparts with prominent yellow rump. Distinctive pale yellow crown stripe and two dark green lateral crown stripes. Long yellow supercilium.. Two white wing bars. Underparts dirty white. Found in high and middle storey in wooded areas.
