Western Osprey
Pandion haliaetus
無危。冬候鳥。中型猛禽，褐、白兩色為主，有粗黑貫眼紋由嘴基延伸至枕部，胸有黑色橫帶，翼面近黑色。見於海岸地方和魚塘上空。
Least Concern. Winter visitors. Medium-sized raptor. Mainly brown and white in colour. Broad black band through eye to nape. Breast band black, upper wings are nearly black. Found in coastal areas and above fish ponds.
