蒼背山雀
Cinereous Tit
Parus cinereus
無危。留鳥。頭黑色，面頰有大塊白斑，胸前總帶着「黑色的領帶」。叫聲變化多端，如獨特的顫抖「磁－磁－」聲，伴有響亮的「即－即－」歌聲。是樹林鳥種, 但亦到耕地活動.
Least Concern. Common Residents. Black head. White patch on cheek. Have black line extending from throat to centre of belly like a "black tie".Various calls, one a distinctive quivering “tzzz-tzzz-” with loud “chik- chik”notes. Woodland tit that visit cultivated land.
