領角鴞
Collared Scops Owl
Otus lettia
無危。留鳥。小型貓頭鷹，主要在夜間活動。虹膜紅褐色，有明顯淡黃褐色領紋，下體深色縱紋疏落。叫聲為輕柔的「Hoot」。見於任何有樹林的環境。
Least Concern. Common Residents. Small eared owl. Common, mainly active at night. Reddish brown iris, pale brownish yellow hind collar. Dark brown back, lighter belly with dark streaks. Larger than Oriental Scops Owl. Call a soft “Hoot”. Found in any wooded habitats.
