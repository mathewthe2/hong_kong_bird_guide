雙斑柳鶯
Two-barred Warbler
Phylloscopus plumbeitarsus
無危。秋季不常見過境遷徙鳥,冬侯鳥。頭頂和上體深橄欖色，眉紋長和黃色，黑色眼線及顯眼的翼紋。喉部，胸部及脇部淺灰色。見於灌木叢及林地。
Least Concern. Uncommon Autumn Passage Migrant, Winter Visitor. Dark olive-green crown and upperparts.with long yellow supercilia,dark eyestripe and prominent wingbar. Whitish below.Washed grey on throat, breast and flanks. Found in shrubland and woodland.
